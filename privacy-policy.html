Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ruan Roo Publishing ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects any information when you visit our website. We are committed to ensuring your privacy is protected.

1. Who We Are

Ruan Roo Publishing is an independent book publisher selling word search books, coloring books, and activity books exclusively through Amazon KDP. You can contact us at rooruan00@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

This website is a static site with no server-side database. We do not collect, store, or process personal data on our servers. The following limited data interactions occur:

  • Contact & enquiry forms: When you submit a message via our contact form or personalisation request, your details are sent directly to our email via your own email client using mailto:. We do not store this data on our website.
  • Reviews: Any reviews you submit are stored only in your own browser's localStorage — on your device. This data never leaves your browser and is not transmitted to us or any third party.
  • Saved books: Books you "save" are stored in your browser's localStorage on your device only.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

We do not use tracking cookies. The website uses your browser's localStorage solely to remember your saved books and any reviews you have written. This data is stored locally on your device and is never sent to us. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website links to and uses the following third-party services:

  • Amazon.com: All book purchases are made on Amazon. When you click "Get Your Copy", you are taken to Amazon's website, which has its own privacy policy. We are not responsible for Amazon's data practices. Please review Amazon's Privacy Notice.
  • Google Fonts: We load fonts (Fredoka One, Nunito) from Google Fonts. This causes your browser to make a request to Google's servers. Please review Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Pinterest: Our website links to our Pinterest page. Pinterest has its own privacy policy. Please review Pinterest's Privacy Policy.

We do not receive any personal data from these third parties as a result of your visit to our site.

5. Email Communications

If you contact us by email at rooruan00@gmail.com or via our contact form, we will use the information you provide solely to respond to your enquiry. We will not share your email address with third parties or add you to any mailing list without your explicit consent.

6. Children's Privacy

Some of our books are designed for young children. However, our website is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information to us, please contact us at rooruan00@gmail.com and we will take steps to remove it.

7. Links to Other Websites

Our website contains links to Amazon and social media platforms. Once you leave our site, we have no control over and accept no responsibility for the content or privacy practices of those websites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not store your personal data on our servers, there is no personal data held by us to access, correct, or delete. If you have emailed us and wish for that correspondence to be deleted, please contact us at rooruan00@gmail.com and we will action your request promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
